## 2. Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
The "Mill Spirit Circle" - circular badge featuring Alberta landscape with waterwheel and mill icons.

#### Color Variations
- **Full Color:** Preferred for most applications
- **Monochrome:** For single-color printing or dark backgrounds
- **Flat:** Simplified for small sizes or digital icons
- **Line Art:** For engraving or minimal applications

#### Sizing
- **Minimum Size:** 0.5" (print) / 100px (digital)
- **Clear Space:** 10% of logo diameter around all sides
- **Scaling:** Always proportional; never distort

#### Placement
- **Background:** Works best on white/light backgrounds; effective on navy or forest green
- **Positioning:** Avoid placing over busy images; ensure contrast

### Logo Lockups

#### Horizontal Lockup
```
[Circle Logo] [Company Name]
                    [Tagline]
```

#### Stacked Lockup
```
   [Circle Logo]
[Company Name]
   [Tagline]
```

#### Standalone Circle
For app icons, favicons, social media avatars.

## 4. Typography

### Primary Fonts

#### Headline Font (Display)
- **Font Family:** Montserrat
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Bold (700)
- **Usage:** Logo, headlines, hero text
- **Characteristics:** Clean, geometric, modern

#### Body Font (Text)
- **Font Family:** Inter
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)
- **Usage:** Body text, documents, web content
- **Characteristics:** Highly readable, neutral, friendly

### Typography Rules

#### Hierarchy
- **H1:** Montserrat Bold, 48pt, #1C2833
- **H2:** Montserrat Bold, 36pt, #2874A6
- **H3:** Montserrat Medium, 24pt, #27AE60
- **Body:** Inter Regular, 16pt, #1C2833
- **Caption:** Inter Regular, 12pt, #A0826D

#### Spacing
- Line height: 1.5x font size minimum
- Letter spacing: 0-5% for headlines, 0% for body

## 6. Visual Elements

### Imagery Style
- **Natural:** Rivers, mountains, landscapes (Alberta-inspired)
- **Technical:** Clean machinery, flowing water, geometric patterns
- **Historic:** Waterwheels, mills, traditional engineering
- **Modern:** Sleek interfaces, data visualizations, sustainable tech

### Photography Guidelines
- High contrast, natural lighting
- Focus on water in motion, natural environments
- Include people using/interacting with systems
- Avoid cluttered or urban settings

### Iconography
- Consistent with logo: geometric, nature-inspired
- Use for web, apps, infographics
- Maintain 2px stroke weight minimum

## 7. Application Guidelines

### Digital Applications
- **Website:** Full color logo in header, consistent typography
- **Social Media:** Circle logo for profile pictures, horizontal lockup for posts
- **Email:** Horizontal lockup in signature, consistent colors

### Print Applications
- **Business Cards:** Horizontal lockup, contact info in Inter
- **Brochures:** Full color logo, consistent color palette
- **Packaging:** Durable materials, weather-resistant inks

### Environmental Considerations
- Use recycled papers, soy-based inks
- Minimize waste in printing
- Digital-first where possible

## 8. Do's and Don'ts

### Logo Usage - Do's
- ✅ Use approved color variations
- ✅ Maintain minimum size and clear space
- ✅ Scale proportionally
- ✅ Use on brand-approved backgrounds

### Logo Usage - Don'ts
- ❌ Alter colors without approval
- ❌ Distort or stretch the logo
- ❌ Add effects (shadows, glows) not in original
- ❌ Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- ❌ Rotate or flip the logo

### General Brand - Do's
- ✅ Use approved fonts and colors
- ✅ Maintain consistent voice and messaging
- ✅ Test accessibility of all materials
- ✅ Get approval for new applications

### General Brand - Don'ts
- ❌ Use unapproved fonts or colors
- ❌ Alter taglines or key messages
- ❌ Create new logo variations without design team
- ❌ Use brand elements in offensive contexts
