## 1. INTRODUCTION

MicroHydro Inc. is seeking proposals for the supply of components for a biomimetic microhydro power system. The system integrates hydro, solar, wind, and battery storage for reliable 24/7 renewable energy.

**Project Overview:**
- Target Output: 10-15 kW peak, 7-10 kW average
- Design Life: 25+ years
- Volume: Initial 10 units, scaling to 50+ annually
- Location: Alberta, Canada (preference for local suppliers)

## 2. SCOPE OF WORK

### Required Components

#### A. Hydro Turbine & Generator
- **Quantity:** 10 units (initial)
- **Specifications:**
  - Crossflow turbine, 5-15m head
  - Ceramic blades, stainless housing
  - Direct-drive PMSG generator, 10kW
  - Water-cooled, corrosion-resistant
- **Delivery:** March 2026

- **Quantity:** 10 units
