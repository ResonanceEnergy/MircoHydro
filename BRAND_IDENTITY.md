- Top: Viktor Schauberger (Waterwheel) - Natural wisdom, vortex dynamics
- Right: Nikola Tesla (Tesla Coil) - Electromagnetic innovation
- Bottom: RWR Module (Magnetic Array) - Proprietary magnetic structuring

- Soft blue gradient

- Blue gradient, darker than sky
